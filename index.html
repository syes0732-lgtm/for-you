from me

some words are better written than said...

to the one who changed everything —

I didn't think one month could feel like this.

Like someone quietly rearranged all the furniture in my chest while I wasn't looking, and now everything sits differently... warmer, a little more sure of itself.

You did that. I don't know if you know but you did.

I look forward to things differently now. September feels like a promise I keep making to myself, and October feels like the exhale at the end of a very long breath. I think about what it'll be like to finally see you. Not on a screen, not through a phone, just you, in the same air as me and I can't think about it too long or I get embarrassingly emotional about it.(I probably will cry when I see you..haha)

One month. And somehow you're already someone I can't imagine not having. You make ordinary days feel like they matter. You make distance feel survivable. You make me feel like the most seen person in any room, even when the room is just a call window at 2am.

I love you. I love who you are, and who I am when I'm talking to you, and all the months and moments that are still ahead of us.

Thank you for trusting me enough to let me see every side of you, especially the ones you don't show everyone else.

Thank you for being mine.
with everything I have, always — yours ✦
